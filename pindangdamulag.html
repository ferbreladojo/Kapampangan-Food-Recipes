Pindang Damulag

Pindang Damulag is a Kapampangan delicacy… what the Tagalogs might refer to as Tosinong Kalabaw… Tocino is the Filipino term for any cured meat.

Recipes

  • Carabao meet
  • Sugar
  • Sukang Sasa

Procedures

  1. Slice the carabao meet into tocino slice.
  2. Add sugar and sukang sasa in the slice carabao and then wait it over night to tick the flavor.
  3. Cook the carabao meet in to a hot cooking oil an then wait until it reddish.
  4. After you see it reddish its ready to serve.
